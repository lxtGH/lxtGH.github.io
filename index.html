I am a Research Scientist at Samsung AI Center, Cambridge. I was awarded Ph.D degree in computer science from Queen Mary University of London in 2018. Prior to joining SAIC, I was a Postdoctoral Researcher at the University of Oxford where I was supervised by Philip H.S. Torr and Andrew Zisserman.
