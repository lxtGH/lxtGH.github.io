I am a PhD student from the Schoole of Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences, Peking University since 2017. I started my PhD study at Peking University after I finished my bachelor degree for Beijing University of Post and Telecommunication. My advisor is Prof. Yunhai Tong. In 2018-2019, I was a research intern mentored by Dr.Kuiyuan Yang at DeepMotion Perception Group where I started my first research. Now, I am a research intern at SenseTime mentored by Dr.Guangliang Cheng and Dr.Jianping Shi.

My recent works are mainly on segmentation including semantic segmentation, panoptic segmentation, video object segmentation.
